About Us

BEATS BY CAYDE is a clan playing Destiny 2 on PS4. We primarily focus on PVE gamemodes like Escalation protocols, nightfalls, and raids, however alot of us enjoy some good old fashion pvp as well .

Why choose BEATS BY CAYDE?

BEATS BY CAYDE is a growing, mid-sized clan with a healthy active roster full of people who enjoy playing destiny . Our goal is to make friends and help others enjoy this game too.

Our members are strong players, but we're not gods. That means that you don't have to be a god to join (but if you are, awesome), but we are not a carry clan.

FAQ

How long has this clan been around?

BEATS BY CAYDE was founded in the first weeks following the release of Destiny 1. Some of the members have been playing ever since the alpha. The original clan name was Subsidizers, but as the clan grew the Admins decided it was time for a name change so when rise of iron came out the name was officially changed to Luminous avant-gardeians. After the release of curse of osiris alot of our members stopped playing, however with the release of warmind and foresaken alot of them have since returned. The name BEAT BY CAYDE was a result of a clan merger with ghost of saint 14 . THE NAME IS OBVIOUSLY AN HOMAGE TO THE LATE GREAT CAYDE-6

Where is the clan located?

BEATS BY CAYDE is a primarily North American clan. We're not opposed to EU members, but you might have a harder time scheduling with other members.

What are the clan rules?

  1. Know where the line is and don't cross it. We joke around, we roast each other, we have a sense of humor, but don't take it too far. Malicious trolling and bullying are not okay here.
  2. Don't be toxic. Overt racism, homophobia, and other hate speech have no place in this clan. Just be chill.
